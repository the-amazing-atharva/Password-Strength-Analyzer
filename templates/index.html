Entropy Logic:

  • Theoretical Entropy: Maximum possible strength based purely on length and characters.
  • Effective Entropy: Real-world strength after the engine subtracts points for patterns (dictionary words, dates, keyboard walks).
  • Formula: We convert the total guesses required into bits using $log_2(Guesses)$.
